Delicious Apple Pie Recipe

Ingredients Instructions

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, chilled and diced
  • 4-5 apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour and granulated sugar. Add the butter and combine until crumbly.
  3. Press the dough into a pie dish. Bake for 20 minutes or until lightly browned.
  4. In a bowl, mix sliced apples, brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Place the mixture into the pie crust.
  5. Bake for about 30-40 minutes or until the apples are soft and the crust is golden brown.
